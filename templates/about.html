About MediSum AI
MediSum AI is an intelligent healthcare platform designed to simplify complex medical reports using Artificial Intelligence, NLP, dashboard analytics, and conversational AI assistance.

Our Mission

MediSum AI helps patients and healthcare professionals understand lengthy medical reports quickly and efficiently. The system uses AI-powered summarization, severity analysis, conversational chatbot assistance, and interactive dashboard analytics to improve healthcare accessibility and patient understanding.

The project combines modern AI technologies with cloud deployment to provide a smart, scalable, and user-friendly healthcare solution.

AI Summarization

Automatically converts long medical reports into concise and understandable summaries.

Conversational AI

Smart chatbot support for answering healthcare-related questions in natural language.

Dashboard Analytics

Interactive charts and healthcare insights for better understanding and monitoring.

Cloud Deployment

Securely accessible online healthcare platform with real-time functionality.
