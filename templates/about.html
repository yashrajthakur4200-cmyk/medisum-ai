About MediSum

MediSum is an NLP-powered system designed to automatically summarize long medical reports into clear and meaningful insights. It supports PDF, text files, and direct text input processing.

What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) enables computers to understand and process human language, supporting automated text analysis and interpretation.

Medical Summarization

MediSum applies TF-IDF and cosine similarity to extract key sentences from lengthy reports and generate high-quality summaries.

Why Hospitals Need It

Summaries allow doctors to review records faster, reduce workload, enhance decision-making, and improve patient care efficiency.

Project Objective

To build a lightweight and effective summarization framework capable of extracting keywords, entities, and quality scoring from medical text.

Scope of the System

Supports text extraction from PDF/TXT, OCR for scanned documents, entity recognition, word statistics, and summary downloads.

Advantages

Efficient, fast, minimal resources, easy deployment, user-friendly design, suitable for hospitals, clinics, and academic research.
